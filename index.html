About Press Start Gaming

Server Specifications

Our dedicated server CPU is an Intel Core i9 9900K with 64GB DDR4 RAM and 1 TB PCIe NVMe storage. The server is housed in a secure datacenter located in Miami, Florida. Looking for dependable server hosting? Visit PSG Hosting.

Support PSG

Press Start Gaming is 100% community funded. Visit our Patreon to become a supporter. You can also support us by shopping at our merch store or making a one-time donation on PayPal. All proceeds from donations and purchases are put directly back into the community and we release periodic financial reports on our Discord for transparency.

Community Events

We host social gaming events daily that are streamed on our Twitch channel, such as oganized operations in Foxhole, custom game types and mods in Squad and other games, and our weekly Dungeons & Dragons campaign sessions.
